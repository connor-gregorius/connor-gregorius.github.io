Why Visit?

Seattle is one of the most popular cities in the entire US and for good reason! Its beautiful scenery and lively atmosphere make it a must-see for any traveler! With countless places to hike, to eat, and to tour, there is something for everyone! Itâ€™s well worth driving a few hours outside the city to explore the wilderness. Seattle has access to mountains, oceans, a rainforest, deserts, farmland, islands, and even a volcano. There are also plenty of art exhibits for those with an artistic mind. Other than Chihuly Garden and Glass, Seattle boasts almost 200 art galleries, five art museums and numerous public art projects scattered across the city. Lastly, the San Juan Islands are the best place to spot whales, especially orcas. The largest of the San Juan Islands is actually named Orcas Island. Keep your eyes peeled on a ferry across the Puget Sound or set up a proper whale-watching tour!
