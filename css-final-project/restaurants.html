Types of Restaurants

Seattle is proud to offer countless varying restaurants to fit everyone's taste. From seafood to sandwiches, whatever you may be looking for, Seattle has it. Check out sites like Cafe Munir, prices are strikingly low for the shared hummus plates, mezze dips and spreads, meat and vegetable kebabs, and family-style platters. Pizzerias like Delancey and Stoneburner will have your mouth watering upon entering.
