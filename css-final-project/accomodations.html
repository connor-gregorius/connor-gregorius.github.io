Hotels and Accomodations

Traveling with kids and pets? Planning a weekend with friends? How about a romantic getaway? Whatever you want for your stay, you can find it at Seattle’s wide variety of hotels. We have plenty of great accomodations to make your trip as memorable as possible. Everything from the best of the best to pet friendly hotels, you won't be left sleepless in Seattle! The city’s diverse lodging options range from historic and elegant accommodations in the heart of the city to hotels with urban art and sleek designs. Many Seattle hotels feature fantastic restaurants and bars, while others are steps from famous sights like the calm waters of Elliott Bay and the iconic Space Needle.
